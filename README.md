## F1 Monza 2024 Simulation ##


A p5.js simulation of Monza 2024 (laps 48–51) condensed into one lap. Due to limited data on driver positions and overtakes, driver speeds were averaged and adjusted to match final standings, creating an approximate visualization of the race’s closing stages.
